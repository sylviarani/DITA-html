What you'll need

For a single-serve sandwich, you'll want to have a single-egg pan. These are available at most stores and they are about 4-5 inches in diameter. A larger pan would work in a pinch, but you may end up with a fried egg that is too big for your bread. You will also need a toaster and a stove.
  • Warning
    Toasters and stoves can be dangerous if used improperly and functionalities vary by model. Be sure to refer to the manufacturer's guidelines for safe use. Always make sure your appliances are clean and functional. Do not leave your toaster or stove unattended. Supervise children closely when appliances are on.
