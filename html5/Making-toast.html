Making toast

Toasters and stoves can be dangerous if used improperly and functionalitues vary by model. Be sure to refer to the manufacturer's guidelines for safe use.
  1. Put your bread in the toaster.
  2. Turn on the toaster.
    For a nice golden-brown, you may want to use setting 2 or 3. Toaster strength can vary greatly, however, so use the setting that works best on yours.
  3. While you wait for the toast, fry your egg.
